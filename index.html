About Me

Hey there! I'm a learning developer who loves exploring the endless world of code and creativity. I'm always experimenting with new tools, frameworks, and ideas—not because I have to, but because I genuinely enjoy it. Whether it's building a small web app or just messing around with animations, I find joy in watching things come to life on screen.

Most of what I do starts with curiosity. I’ll come across something interesting—maybe a piece of UI, a text effect, or a random idea—and I just dive in. It’s never about being perfect; it’s about learning, playing, and improving a little every day. That process of figuring things out is what keeps me going.

I especially love working with anything that involves text, visuals, or interactive elements. There’s something really satisfying about turning simple lines of code into something people can see and interact with. This site is part of that journey—a space where I can keep learning, building, and sharing the things I create, just for the fun of it.
